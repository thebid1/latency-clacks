## The Concept

Traditional NFT auctions have a major flaw: **bid visibility**. When bids are public, sniping and psychological manipulation distort price discovery. Sellers don't get fair market value, and buyers pay more than necessary.

Our solution uses **Arcium MPC** (Multi-Party Computation):
- Bids are encrypted using Arcium's confidential computing network
- No single party can see bids until reveal phase
- Computation happens on encrypted data
- True sealed-bid mechanics on a public blockchain
